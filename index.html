About Me

Hello, I’m Jinyue Xia, a Ph.D. student in UNC Charlotte(expected graduation 2015). My research field is Human-Computer Interaction(HCI). My research is focusing on exploring the design of multi-video interaction, user engagement of video and video collaboratory. I'm very glad to work with Dr. David C. Wilson (My advisor). I am a member of HCI Lab in the Department of Software and Information Systems.
