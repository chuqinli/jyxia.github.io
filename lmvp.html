Lightweight Multi-Video Player

Solutions:
Javascript, jQuery, Popcorn.js, HTML5/CSS

LMVP supports multiple videos to play at a time. This research project focuses on exploring design space of multiple video interaction.

Features

  1. Support Youtube, Vimeo, HTML5 videos
  2. Easy video selection: Drag and drop
  3. Easy video segmentation selection
  4. Globle volume/playback control
  5. ...
